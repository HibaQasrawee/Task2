Alf Laila W Laila

An Middle Eastern restaurant boasts an elegant, classic decor and a spacious area, creating a comfortable atmosphere suitable for families and groups. When you visit the restaurant, you can enjoy a variety of delicious Middle Eastern appetizers, such as hummus, baba ghanoush, pickles, and olives, in addition to salads like tabbouleh, fattoush, and arugula salad. As for the main dishes, they offer a diverse range of grilled dishes, stews such as salmon stew, mansaf, lamb saj, as well as pizza, pasta, and burgers. They also feature special Friday dishes like stuffed lamb neck, grilled lamb, grape leaves, and many other delectable dishes.

Features of "Alf Layla wa Layla" Restaurant

  • Free Entry
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Family-Friendly
  • Disability-Friendly
  • Suitable for Groups
  • Indoor Seating Areas
